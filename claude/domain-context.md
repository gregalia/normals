# Domain Context (Optometry Practice)

## Typical Patient Flow

1. **Registration/Check-in** - Verify insurance, update demographics
2. **Pre-exam** - Visual acuity, autorefraction, tonometry (eye pressure)
3. **Clinical Exam** - Refraction, retinal examination, prescription determination
4. **Post-exam** - Frame selection, contact lens fitting, follow-up scheduling
5. **Checkout** - Payment processing, insurance claims, appointment scheduling

## Key Clinical Data

- **Visual Acuity** (20/20, 20/40, etc.)
- **Refraction** (sphere, cylinder, axis for each eye)
- **Intraocular Pressure** (glaucoma screening)
- **Retinal Photos** and diagnostic images
- **Contact Lens** specifications and fit assessments
- **Frame** measurements and adjustments

## Regulatory Requirements

- **HIPAA Compliance** - Patient data encryption, access logging, consent management
- **Prescription Validity** - Expiration dates, controlled substance tracking
- **Insurance Requirements** - Prior authorizations, claim documentation
- **State Licensing** - Provider credentials, scope of practice validation

## Security Considerations

- **Encryption at rest** for all patient data
- **Encryption in transit** for all service communication
- **Role-based access control** (doctors, technicians, reception staff)
- **Audit logging** for all patient data access
- **Session management** with appropriate timeouts
- **Two-factor authentication** for sensitive operations
